Who we are

A Uganda-based multimedia production company

We specialize in documentary production, television, photography, videography, drone filming, digital storytelling, journalism, media training, and youth empowerment — with a focus on education, tourism, climate change, culture, and community transformation.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading storytelling and youth empowerment media organization that inspires positive social and economic transformation.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality media content while empowering youth through practical skills training, mentorship, innovation, and employment opportunities.

SM

Samuel Musana

Founder & Managing Director

Samuel Musana is an award-winning media practitioner, filmmaker, photographer, drone pilot, and development communicator with extensive experience in multimedia storytelling, documentary production, climate reporting, tourism promotion, and youth empowerment initiatives.

As the founder of KESH MEDIA PRODUCTIONS LTD, he leads the organization's strategic vision, partnerships, project implementation, and creative direction while mentoring young media professionals across Uganda.
